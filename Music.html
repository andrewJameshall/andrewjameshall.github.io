Andrew J Hall (BMus)

Performer, composer, arranger, and producer

Studying performance jazz Saxophone at the New Zealand School of Music, Andrew Hall (Bmus) is versatile performer, composer, arranger, and producer based in Auckland. Specializing in saxophone, clarinet, flute, and vocals, Andrew was formerly a member of dDub and now leads his own band, Spiral Collective. He also holds the chair of lead alto saxophone and arranges and composes for the Auckland Jazz Orchestra. Andrew has performed with artists like Eric Marienthal, Chris Thompson, Georgie Fame, Rodger Fox, and Dave Matthews, and has appeared at festivals such as the Monterey Jazz Festival and Melbourne Fringe Festival. He has recorded on 20 albums, composed and arranged 15 works for jazz orchestra and produced 3 albums. He currently runs his own studio Welsh Hills Studios,

Instruments:

  • Saxophone (Alto Tenor Soprano Baritone)
  • Clarinet
  • Flute
  • Voice
  • Secondary instruments: Guitar, Bass, Piano and Trumpet

Skills for Hire:

  • Performance in multiple bands in Jazz Funk and Blues
  • Arranging for Big Band and Horn Sections
  • Recording and Producing

Spiral Collective

Where soul meets samba, rock and jazz meet reggae, and horns rub shoulders with lush keys and broken strings, these are the places where Spiral unites. A collective of some of New Zealand's finest musicians. With all of the spontaneity, freedom and musical interplay with a unique Kiwi twist.

Auckland Jazz Orchestra

uckland Jazz Orchestra exists to perform original music by New Zealand composers and arrangers. Formed in 2009, the Auckland Jazz Orchestra has the unique mission of playing solely New Zealand composed or arranged music, in a contemporary jazz style. Comprised of professional musicians and talented graduates. The band performs ompositions an arrangements by Andrew Hall, Tim Atkinson, and band leader Mike Booth.
