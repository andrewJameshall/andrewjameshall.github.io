Kia Ora, Welcome!

I am a Royal Society Mana Tūānuku Research Leader Fellow and a Senior Research Fellow and Senior Lecturer of Mechanical Engineering at the University of Auckland, New Zealand. I am a translational researcher focused on using utilising fundamental science and engineering to control sound and vibration. I am also a proffesional musician, arranger, composer and producer, and a passionate photographer capturing moments and enviroments from around the world.
